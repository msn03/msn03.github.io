About the Project

Anti Social Media Club is a site whose mission is to inform about the impact of social networks on people. It also allows you to check yourself how addicted to social networks you are.

The information is taken from reliable sources, from statistical centers or experienced experts. Links to sources can be seen below.

Psychological Quizes mind.help
